The LEGACY

"History has been kind to us, because we have always intended to create it."
Ramani Icecream Company Limited has been serving scoops filled with magic, with smiles for almost 48 plus glorious years now. Our history dates back to a humble beginning that was a thought dreamt by Late Shri Balchand Kukreja in 1969. The reins of the business are now held by his five grandsons who further developed the empire to what it stands today.
